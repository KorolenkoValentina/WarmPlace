About Us

Warm Place manufactures fireplaces, stoves, braziers, and barbecue grills with direct delivery and professional installation at your home.

Our products stand out thanks to durable materials, careful workmanship, and fair pricing that keeps our customers satisfied. The Warm Place approach is simple: reliable quality at a reasonable price.

We also deliver our equipment to other regions, and our couriers make sure every item arrives safely and intact before installation.

From dispatch to final setup, we focus on a smooth and secure customer experience.
